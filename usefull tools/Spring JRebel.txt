Ściągamy dependency:
<dependency>
    <groupId>org.springframework.boot</groupId>
    <artifactId>spring-boot-devtools</artifactId>
    <optional>true</optional>
</dependency>

W IntellijIdea:
Help > Find Action > Registry > compiler.automake.allow.when.app.running and check
Nastêpnie zanzaczamy project:
File > Settings > Compiler > build project automatically
